<ul>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/accountant-lawyer-alliance/">Accountant-Lawyer Alliance (ALA)</a></strong>&nbsp;is a member association built to overcome the obstacles of modern accountants and legal professionals.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/american-accounting-association/">American Accounting Association (AAA)</a></strong>&nbsp;is the largest community of accountants in academia.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/american-institute-of-certified-public-accountants/">American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA)</a></strong> is the national, professional organization for all CPAs.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/american-payroll-association/">American Payroll Association (APA)</a></strong> is the nation’s leader in payroll education, publications, and training for payroll professionals.</li>
<li><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/american-society-of-cost-segregation-professionals/"><strong>American Society of Cost Segregation Professionals (ASCSP)</strong></a>&nbsp;establishes a measurable standard by which cost segregation consultants can be evaluated.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/association-of-accounting-and-finance-professionals/">Association of Accounting and Finance Professionals (ANAFP)</a></strong> is the largest professional association dedicated solely to those working in and/or interested in the field of nonprofit accounting and finance.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/association-of-chartered-certified-system-accountants/">Association of Chartered Certified System Accountants (ACCSA)</a></strong> is a membership organization for accounting professionals within an IT environment.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/institute-of-internal-auditors/">Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA)</a></strong> is the internal audit profession’s global voice, recognized authority, acknowledged leader, chief advocate, and principal educator.</li>
<li><span class="lt-line-clamp__line"><strong><a href="https://www.imanet.org/">Institute of Management Accountants (IMA)</a></strong> is one of</span>&nbsp;<span class="lt-line-clamp__line">the largest and most respected associations focused exclusively on advancing the</span>&nbsp;<span class="lt-line-clamp__line lt-line-clamp__line--last">management accounting profession.</span></li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-association-of-certified-public-bookkeepers/">National Association of Certified Public Bookkeepers (NACPB)</a></strong> serves bookkeepers providing bookkeeping services to the public.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-association-of-estate-planners-councils/">National Association of Estate Planners &amp; Councils (NAEPC)</a></strong> is a multi-disciplinary membership organization comprised of estate planners and estate planning councils for excellence in estate planning.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-association-of-enrolled-agents/">National Association of Enrolled Agents (NAEA)</a></strong> is the professional society representing tax preparers who have earned the distinction of “enrolled agent” (EA).</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-association-of-professional-accountants/">National Association of Professional Accountants (NAPA)</a></strong>&nbsp;trains, certifies, and licenses bookkeeping students, employees, and professionals.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-association-of-tax-professionals/">National Association of Tax Professionals (NATP)</a></strong>&nbsp;provides its members the support, education,<wbr> products and services needed to succeed in the tax profession<wbr>.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-conference-of-cpa-practitioners/">National Conference of CPA Practitioners (ncCPAp)</a></strong> is a membership organization advocating on issues that affect CPAs.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-society-of-accountants/">National Society of Accountants (NSA)</a></strong>&nbsp;is the association for main street tax and accounting professionals.</li>
<li id="extended-description-modal-header"><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/professional-association-of-small-business-accountants/">Professional Association of Small Business Accountants (PASBA)</a></strong>&nbsp;represents CPAs, public accountants, and enrolled agents who provide accounting services to small businesses.</li>
<li><span data-sheets-value="{" data-sheets-userformat="{"><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/tax-executives-institute/">Tax Executives Institute (TEI)</a></strong></span>&nbsp;is the professional association representing the in-house tax professional community.</li>
</ul>
