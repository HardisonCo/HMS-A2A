<div class="fusion-text fusion-text-1"><h3 data-fontsize="30" style="--fontSize: 30; line-height: 1.3;" data-lineheight="39px" class="fusion-responsive-typography-calculated">List of Administrative Professional Associations &amp; Organizations</h3>
<p>The following Administrative Professional Associations &amp; Organizations offer networking and professional development opportunities specifically for secretaries, receptionists, and executive administrative assistants.</p>
<p>Membership benefits of joining include the ability to connect with like-minded professionals and stay abreast of trends and happenings within this field. Becoming a member is also a great way to bolster your credentials by displaying them on your Resume and LinkedIn profile.</p>
<p><strong>Read: <a href="https://jobstars.com/how-to-build-your-network-using-professional-associations/">How to Build Your Network Using Professional Associations</a></strong></p>
<p>These membership-based organizations are dedicated to serving the interests of professionals within a particular specialization or niche. It’s wise to visit each association’s website to get more information on specializations, membership costs, and requirements.</p>
<h5 data-fontsize="18" style="--fontSize: 18; line-height: 1.5; --minFontSize: 18;" data-lineheight="27px" class="fusion-responsive-typography-calculated">Here’s the List:</h5>
<ul>
<li><strong style="font-size: 16px;"><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/american-society-of-administrative-professionals/">American Society of Administrative Professionals (ASAP)</a></strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">&nbsp;provides professional development, training, and resources for&nbsp;administrative professionals and executive assistants.</span></li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/association-of-executive-and-administrative-professionals/">Association of Executive and Administrative Professionals (AEAP)</a>&nbsp;</strong>helps<strong>&nbsp;</strong>members stay informed of advances and changes in professional practice and technology.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/executive-assistants-organization/">Executive Assistants Organization (EAO)</a></strong> is a globally recognized association for assistants with a desire to achieve EA stardom<strong>.</strong></li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/c-suite-executive-support-professionals/">Executive Support Professionals (ESP)</a></strong> is a small community of administrative professionals determined to advance the administrative community.</li>
<li><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/international-association-of-administrative-professionals/"><strong>International Association of Administrative Professionals (IAAP)</strong></a> is an association for office professionals with approximately 20,000 members and affiliates.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/international-virtual-assistants-association/">International Virtual Assistants Association (IVAA)</a></strong> is a membership organization dedicated to the professional education and development of members of the virtual assistance profession.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-association-of-presidential-assistants-in-higher-education/">National Association of Presidential Assistants in Higher Education (NAPAHE)</a></strong> serves presidential assistants (PAs) and other professionals supporting higher education leaders.</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://jobstars.com/professional-associations/national-association-of-professional-receptionists/">National Association of Professional Receptionists (NAPR)</a></strong> is a professional association for receptionists, switchboard operators, information clerks, greeters, and administrative professionals nationwide.</li>
</ul>
